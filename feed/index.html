English Translation

The colors have faded from the canvas,
The lines have left the letters,
The melody has slipped from music,
And words that lips can’t dare release now seek the pen and ink to speak.

I took up my journal, tried to weave a piece,
Yet the words would not break free.
No count for each syllable,
Each line drifting freely.

Until I found an image,
Each stroke dark and bold,
A piece that seeks a glimmer of notice.
Chaotic! The ink’s every sweep holds no direction.

My heart races fast, anxious and trembling,
Struggling to stay afloat in a sea of tears,
Battling sorrow’s relentless waves,
My pen as my weapon, my paper as shield, blood as my ink.

These chains binding my mind bear witness to the heavens,
I reach, straining to meet their standards,
Fighting fear and dread alike—
In this war, surrender means death.

Reflecting in the emptiness,
Watching the sky, waiting for the sun’s descent and the moon’s rise,
Feeling the breeze and the waves crashing on the shore.

Night deepens again, a signal to mourn and regret,
Lost, wandering, chasing after myself,
Hiding from judgmental eyes.

I am a captive in a box,
Longing to break free from my self-imposed prison,
I will push forward until I’m freed and leave the past behind.

Demons placed me in this greedy cell,
Evil that could never be quenched.
Thorns lodged in my throat, barriers in my path—
In my freedom, blood will stain the way.

I will swallow the darkness whole,
No obstacle will keep me from life’s challenges;
Even if hell blocks my path, I will enter,
My soul ablaze, burning endlessly.

The battle has begun, no turning back now.
In life’s war, there are only two ends: triumph or death.
The victor stands tall to applause, but the fallen,
Feasted upon by ravens, craving flesh.

Chains that have bound me end here.
I will run, though gasping for breath, shout though my voice goes hoarse,
Endure any storm that comes,
Fight until life’s candle burns to the last.

Now I’ll be freed from this prison,
Though the future is yet unknown,
I’m turning to a new page,
Etching my journey into words.
One day, my struggle will be an inspiration, a tale worth telling.

Cast your secret sorrows to the universe,
Don’t fear the sunset or the coming of dusk.
Darkness doesn’t always bring doom—
Remember, the fire shines brightest in the dark.

When love falls apart, it’s like the ground suddenly disappears from beneath us. Heartbreak hits hard, leaving us stranded in a sea of emotions with no life raft in sight. But in the wreckage of shattered dreams, there’s a chance for something powerful to happen: a journey of self-discovery.

Heartbreak isn’t just about pain; it’s about growth. It’s about learning to navigate through the storm, finding strength in the midst of chaos. Because here’s the thing: the glass isn’t dangerous until it’s broken. And in those broken pieces lies the potential for something beautiful to emerge.

In the aftermath of heartbreak, it’s okay to feel lost and confused. It’s okay to mourn what once was and wonder what could have been. But amidst the tears and the pain, there’s a quiet strength that begins to rise within us, a resilience that says, “You will get through this.”

Heartbreak teaches us the importance of self-love and self-care. It’s a reminder to be gentle with ourselves, to give ourselves the grace and compassion we so freely give to others. Because only when we learn to love ourselves fully can we invite others to love us in return.

And as we journey through the darkness of heartbreak, we find light in the love and support of those around us. Friends who lend a listening ear, family who offer a comforting hug, and strangers who become allies in our healing journey. Their presence reminds us that we are not alone, that there is hope even in the darkest of nights.

So if you find yourself standing in the rubble of a broken heart, know that you are not weak; you are simply human. Embrace the journey with open arms, knowing that with each step forward, you are one step closer to healing. And remember, while the glass may be broken, the beauty lies in how we choose to rebuild.

How to Find the Best Freelance Web Developer for Your Project

Are you struggling to create a website that effectively showcases your business or brand? Do you feel like your website is outdated and doesn’t meet your customers’ needs? It’s time to hire the best freelance web developer to take your website to the next level. A skilled freelance web developer can create a website that not only looks great but also functions smoothly and engages your customers.

Hiring a freelance web developer can be a daunting task, especially if you’re not well-versed in the world of web development. It’s important to find someone who not only has the technical skills to bring your vision to life but also understands your business needs and goals. 

 In this article, we will discuss why hiring the best freelance web developer is crucial for your business and how to find the perfect match for your project.

What is a Freelance Web Developer?

Before we dive into how to find the best freelance web developer, it’s important to understand what a freelance web developer is. A freelance web developer is a self-employed individual who offers web development services to clients on a project-by-project basis. Unlike a web development agency, a freelance web developer typically works independently and can offer more affordable rates.

What makes the best freelance web developer?

Determine Your Needs

Before you start looking for a freelance web developer, you need to determine your website’s requirements. What is the purpose of your website? Who is your target audience? What features do you need? Answering these questions will help you find the right web developer for your project. You may also want to consider the budget and timeline for your project.

Look for Experienced Web Developers

When looking for a freelance web developer, experience is crucial. You want someone who has a track record of creating successful websites for clients in your industry. Look for a web developer with a portfolio of previous work, which should include examples of sites that are similar to what you want.

Creativity:

A great web developer should be able to bring your vision to life with their creativity. They should be able to create a unique design that sets your website apart from the rest. In addition, a great web developer should have a keen eye for detail. They should be able to spot and fix any errors or bugs in your website before it goes live.

Check for Technical Skills

A good web developer should have a strong technical background. They should be proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other web development technologies. They should also be familiar with content management systems like WordPress, Drupal, and Joomla.

Communication Skills

Effective communication is key to a successful web development project. When evaluating potential freelance web developers, pay attention to their communication skills. Do they respond promptly to your emails or phone calls? Are they able to explain technical concepts in a way that’s easy for you to understand?

Testimonials and Reviews

Testimonials and reviews are a great way to gauge the quality of a freelance web developer’s work. Look for reviews on their website, social media pages, and other online platforms. You may also want to ask for references from their previous clients.

Cost and Timeline

Cost and timeline are two critical factors to consider when hiring a freelance web developer. While you may be tempted to choose the cheapest option, keep in mind that quality should be your top priority. You should also discuss the timeline for your project with your web developer and ensure that they can meet your deadlines.

Contract and Payment Terms

Once you have found the right freelance web developer for your project, it’s essential to have a contract in place. The contract should include project scope, payment terms, and timelines. Make sure that you understand the contract’s terms before signing it.

In conclusion, finding the right freelance web developer for your project requires a bit of research and due diligence. Take the time to determine your needs, look for experienced web developers with technical skills, and check their communication skills, testimonials, and reviews. Consider the cost and timeline for your project and ensure that you have a contract in place before starting.

Digital Marketing in 2023

In the past few years, digital marketing has evolved rapidly, and this trend is expected to continue in 2023. As more and more people spend time online, businesses are increasingly looking to reach their target audience through digital channels.

Here are the key trends and strategies to expect in digital marketing this year:

  1. Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) AI and ML are set to play a significant role in digital marketing in 2023. These technologies will help businesses automate their marketing tasks and provide personalized experiences to their customers.

  2. Video Marketing Video content is expected to be the dominant form of content in 2023. Businesses should focus on creating engaging video content to reach and engage with their target audience.

  3. Voice Search Optimization With the increasing popularity of voice-activated devices such as Amazon Echo and Google Home, voice search optimization is becoming increasingly important. Businesses should optimize their websites and content for voice search to stay ahead of the competition.

  4. Influencer Marketing Influencer marketing is expected to continue to grow in popularity, as consumers trust recommendations from influencers more than traditional advertising.

  5. Interactive Content Interactive content, such as quizzes, polls, and interactive videos, is expected to become more popular in 2023. This type of content can help businesses increase engagement and conversions.

  6. Micro-Moments Micro-moments refer to the short, intense moments when consumers expect immediate information or action from their device. Businesses should be prepared to provide relevant and helpful information in these moments to stay top-of-mind for their target audience.

  7. Customer Data Privacy Data privacy is becoming a major concern for consumers, and businesses must take measures to protect customer data. This includes implementing strict data protection policies and being transparent about how customer data is used.

In conclusion, digital marketing in 2023 is expected to be shaped by AI, video marketing, voice search optimization, influencer marketing, interactive content, micro-moments, and customer data privacy. Businesses that keep up with these trends and adapt their strategies accordingly will be well-positioned to succeed in the digital landscape.

Why Every Business Needs A Website?

A business’s online presence can significantly impact its success, regardless of the industry it belongs to. However, many businesses still do not understand the importance of having a website, which is the primary source of their online presence. Today, a vast majority of customers visit a company’s website before making a purchase.

Having a robust online presence, particularly a website, can make or break a business’s chance of generating more revenue. The quality of the website plays a crucial role in the results, but the purpose of this article is to emphasize the significance of having a website in the first place. As an experienced consultant, I have helped several companies, both big and small, establish their digital presence. In some cases, organizations were hesitant to go online as they felt they were not tech-savvy enough and did not know how to manage a website.

Credibility

Having a website for your business is essential for increasing your organization’s credibility. There are probably several providers offering a similar service as yours. One way to stand out from the crowd is to have a well-designed website that communicates quality information to your customers. Without a website, people may question the legitimacy of your business, but having a website is an opportunity to make a lasting first impression and provide comfort to customers that your business is real.

Brand

A website provides an opportunity to showcase your brand to prospective customers. By clearly establishing your brand identity, what you represent, and what you stand for, you increase the chances of customers buying from you. It also sets you apart from your competitors as it is challenging to communicate your brand effectively without a website. People cannot easily find quality and reliable information about your business without one.

Leads

One of the most compelling reasons to have a website for your business is that it increases the chances of getting leads. Once people find your business online and become interested in your product or service, the information on your website helps them contact you, giving you the opportunity to increase sales. Websites come with a cost, but when used correctly, they have a positive return on investment.

Organic Traffic 

An SEO-optimized website gives you the chance of appearing in Google search results. When people are searching for a product or service, your website may show up in the results, which can drastically increase your customer base.

Saving You Time + Customer Service

Many businesses receive calls from prospects or existing customers asking simple questions about their location and hours of operation. These calls can be time-consuming and distract employees from other important tasks. A website reduces these calls, increases internal productivity, and helps customers find useful information without having to call, which ultimately provides a better user experience.

Updates And Announcements

Since your website is accessible 24/7, it’s easy to post updates and announcements to your customers. This keeps them informed about everything your business is doing and increases the chances of upselling them when something relevant arises.

Digital Marketing

If you plan on leveraging digital marketing to increase leads and grow your business, you will likely want to drive traffic to a website or landing page. An existing website with historical traffic can help you target the most qualified customers and get the best return on investment for your ad spend. Having a website set up in advance is crucial, even if you’re not planning to run ads immediately.

In conclusion, websites have become essential for businesses today, and I strongly recommend creating one if you haven’t already. You can improve it over time, but the most important thing is to start.
