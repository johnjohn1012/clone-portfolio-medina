Meet the leaders of Olsen Commercial

Meet Leaders

Josh Olsen

Josh Olsen is the visionary founder of Olsen Commercial LTD, established in 2008. His company is a global leader in providing quality new and used machinery, with a strong focus on earth moving/compaction, powered machines, vehicles, and trailers. Serving clients worldwide, including Australia, the Pacific Islands, and the USA, Josh sources equipment from New Zealand, specializing in retired lease equipment. What sets Olsen Commercial apart is their exceptional team, boasting outstanding customer service and deep industry expertise, all backed by a robust logistics network. Discover unbeatable prices and top-notch machinery solutions with Josh Olsen's dedicated team.

Ken Stewart

Ken Stewart is the accomplished General Manager at Olsen Commercial Ltd, where he has been instrumental in the company's journey. Olsen Commercial specializes in the global trade of high-quality used machinery, with a notable presence across Oceania, South East Asia, Pacific Islands, the USA, and South America. Ken's expert leadership and strategic acumen have played a pivotal role in the company's success, particularly in efficiently moving equipment across international borders. The company's diverse inventory encompasses everything from robust 1.7 to 25-ton excavators, gensets, and earthmoving machinery to versatile access equipment and small electric hand tools. Olsen Commercial sources its top-notch machinery from New Zealand and Australia, providing customers with competitive prices and exceptional quality.
