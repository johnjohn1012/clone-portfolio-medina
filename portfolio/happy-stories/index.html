Who we are

Happy Stories are a team of creative photographers and videographers specializing Weddings in Sydney mainly Greater West, Hunter Valley, Newcastle, Wollongong, Blue Mountains, Southern Highlands, and also now extending to Melbourne , and Brisbane. Game Plan

Our Wedding Workflow

1. Zoom Chat / Google Meet

Hey there, friend! So, I wanted to fill you in on what happens when we team up for your special day. It all starts with a Zoom date where we really get to know each other. We’ll chat about your love story, your vision for the wedding, and figure out which wedding collection is the perfect fit for you. It’s like a virtual coffee date!

2. Questionnaires
After that, I’ll send you a couple of questionnaires to gather all the nitty gritty details. Things like contact info and a shot list for those family photos. It might seem boring, but trust me, it helps me create a detailed photography timeline. This ensures that we capture all those important moments throughout the day. We’ll go over it together, so no worries, you won’t be left in the dark!

3. Wedding Day

And then, the big day arrives! I’ll be buzzing around, capturing every little magical moment. Seriously, no pressure, but it’s one of the most important days of your life, so we’ll do our absolute best to make sure it’s documented in the most beautiful way possible.

4. Highlights/ Teaser

Now, here’s the exciting part. We’ll send you best 50+ highlights photos within seven days! I know the anticipation can be killer, so I’ll make sure you get a little taste of what’s to come. And then, within four weeks, I’ll deliver the final online gallery. You’ll have full printing and posting rights, so feel free to share those beautiful memories with the world. Plus, if you have wedding video booked in your package, it should be ready within 8 to 12 weeks to share with your families, love ones, and friends and best part is you’ll receive the 60 seconds teaser video within 3 days only! How awesome is that right?

Alright, enough chit chat for now. I’m so excited to be on this journey with you and create some amazing memories together.

Until then,
Gian

Our

Mission

For us, being a photographers and videographers is not just a profession, it’s a calling. It’s about capturing fleeting moments and turning them into timeless treasures. It’s about understanding that love is not static, but an ever-evolving journey. Each couple that steps in front of our cameras brings their unique story, their own adventures and struggles, and we are honored to be a part of that.

Love is not always easy or picture-perfect, and that’s exactly what we strive to capture. The raw, authentic moments that are not staged or posed, but rather the moments that happen naturally. The stolen glances, the tender kisses, and the laughter that fills the air. Those are the moments that make a photographs come alive, that tell a story without words.

But being a photographers and videographers is not just about capturing those moments, it’s also about creating an experience. We want your time with us to be enjoyable, stress-free, and memorable. We believe that the best photographs are taken when you are relaxed and comfortable, allowing your love to shine through effortlessly.

So, we invite you to step into our world, to trust us to capture your love story. We promise you that we will put our hearts and soul into every photograph, ensuring that your unique love is documented just as it is. No filters, no pretense, just the raw and genuine love that you share.
