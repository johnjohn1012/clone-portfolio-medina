    is a multi-brand motorcycle store

    Motostrada is a multi-brand motorcycle store, and is the exclusive distributor of MV AGUSTA and CFMOTO motorcycles. We officially opened to the public on September 2018.
    Our store is a 1500 sqm. facility converted from a 40 year old, family-owned metalworks factory.
    It is located along 8th Street and 11th Avenue on Grace Park, in Caloocan City and is a one-stop shop offering Sales, Spare Parts and Service and Maintenance for the brands it carries.
    The second floor area is the first, exclusive MV Agusta authorized store in the Philippines, and currently stocks rare and limited edition models straight from the MV Agusta factory in Varese, Italy.

    Our retail area has an indoor track, lounge, and waiting area and is adjacent our service section where original     parts for CFMOTO and MV Agusta are readily available.
